ACME CORP — WORK FROM HOME & BENEFITS POLICY 2024
==================================================

SECTION A: WORK FROM HOME (WFH) POLICY
---------------------------------------

A1. ELIGIBILITY
All full-time employees who have completed their probation period (90 days) are eligible for WFH.
Employees on Performance Improvement Plans (PIP) are not eligible for WFH.

A2. WFH ALLOWANCE
Employees may work from home up to 2 days per week.
WFH days must be pre-approved by the reporting manager.
Requests must be submitted at least 24 hours in advance via email or the HR portal.

A3. WFH DURING PROBATION
Employees in their probation period are required to work from the office 5 days a week.
Exception: Special approval from HR Head required.

A4. RESPONSIBILITIES WHILE WFH
Employees must be available on Slack/Teams during standard working hours (9am–6pm).
Must attend all scheduled meetings via video call.
Internet connectivity issues do not excuse non-attendance.

SECTION B: COMPENSATION & SALARY
----------------------------------

B1. SALARY STRUCTURE
Basic Salary: 40% of CTC
House Rent Allowance (HRA): 20% of basic salary
Transport Allowance: Rs. 1,600 per month
Medical Allowance: Rs. 1,250 per month
Special Allowance: Balance of CTC

B2. SALARY DISBURSEMENT
Salaries are credited on the last working day of each month.
Salary slips are available on the HR portal by the 5th of the following month.

B3. PERFORMANCE APPRAISALS
Annual appraisals are conducted in April.
Mid-year performance reviews in October.
Increment letters are issued in May.

SECTION C: HEALTH & WELLNESS BENEFITS
----------------------------------------

C1. MEDICAL INSURANCE
All employees and their immediate family (spouse + 2 children) are covered under group medical insurance.
Sum insured: Rs. 5,00,000 per family per year.
Insurance provider: Star Health Insurance.
Cashless hospitalization available at 5,000+ hospitals across India.

C2. TERM LIFE INSURANCE
All employees are covered under group term life insurance.
Coverage: 3x annual CTC (minimum Rs. 25,00,000).

C3. GRATUITY
Employees are eligible for gratuity after 5 years of continuous service.
Gratuity = (Last drawn basic salary × 15 × years of service) / 26.

SECTION D: PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT
--------------------------------------

D1. LEARNING BUDGET
Each employee receives Rs. 15,000 per year for professional development.
Can be used for courses, certifications, books, or conferences.
Reimbursement requires manager approval and invoice submission.

D2. SPONSORED CERTIFICATIONS
AWS, GCP, Azure, PMP, and other industry certifications are sponsored by the company.
Employee must serve for at least 1 year post-certification or reimburse the cost.
