ACME CORP — LEAVE POLICY 2024
==============================

1. CASUAL LEAVE
Employees are entitled to 12 casual leaves per calendar year.
Casual leaves can be availed in half-day increments (minimum 0.5 days).
Unused casual leaves lapse at the end of the calendar year and cannot be carried forward.
Casual leave requires at least 1 day prior approval from the reporting manager.

2. SICK LEAVE
Employees are entitled to 7 sick leaves per calendar year.
Sick leave can be taken without prior notice in case of sudden illness.
A medical certificate is required for sick leave exceeding 3 consecutive days.
Unused sick leaves can be carried forward to the next year, up to a maximum of 14 days.

3. EARNED LEAVE / PRIVILEGE LEAVE
Employees earn 1 day of earned leave for every 20 days worked.
Maximum accumulation: 30 earned leaves at any time.
Earned leave requires a minimum 7 days advance notice.
Encashment of up to 15 earned leaves is allowed at the end of the year.

4. MATERNITY LEAVE
Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave.
Maternity leave can begin 8 weeks before the expected delivery date.
Documentation: Medical certificate from a registered gynecologist required.

5. PATERNITY LEAVE
Male employees are entitled to 5 working days of paid paternity leave.
Must be taken within 3 months of the child's birth.

6. BEREAVEMENT LEAVE
3 paid days for the death of an immediate family member (spouse, parent, child, sibling).
Proof of relationship may be requested.

7. LEAVE APPLICATION PROCESS
All leave applications must be submitted via the HR portal (hr.acmecorp.com).
Manager approval is mandatory before availing leave.
Retrospective leave applications are not accepted except in medical emergencies.
